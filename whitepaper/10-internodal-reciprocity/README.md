# Section 10 — Internodal Reciprocity

This directory contains Section 10 of the Integral white paper.

Section 9 formalized **when nodes must coordinate** (scope expansion, coordination envelopes, dissolution).
Section 10 formalizes **how nodes cooperate under normal conditions** through non-market reciprocal flows—without expanding governance scope or creating higher authority.
