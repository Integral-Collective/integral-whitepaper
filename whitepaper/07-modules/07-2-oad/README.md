# Open Access Design (OAD)

The Open Access Design (OAD) system is the design intelligence layer of Integral.

It enables the collaborative creation, simulation, validation, optimization, and dissemination of production designs within a shared knowledge commons.

OAD replaces proprietary, market-driven design fragmentation with a transparent, modular, and continuously improving architecture for technological development.

Rather than treating design as private intellectual property, OAD treats design as a **collective adaptive infrastructure** grounded in:

- ecological coherence
- material transparency
- lifecycle awareness
- skill accessibility
- modular reuse
- continuous optimization
- cross-system interoperability

OAD ensures that production knowledge evolves as a shared capability aligned with CDS governance, COS execution, ITC reciprocity, and FRS feedback.
