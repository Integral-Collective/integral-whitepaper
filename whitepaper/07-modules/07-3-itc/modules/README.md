# ITC Modules

This directory contains the detailed module architecture of the Integral Time Credit subsystem.

Each module represents a distinct functional layer within ITC’s contribution recording, access allocation, and reciprocity coordination framework.

---

## Module Overview

1. Labor Event Capture & Verification  
Records and validates contribution events across cooperative contexts.

2. Skill & Context Weighting Engine  
Applies contextual weighting to labor signals without enabling hierarchy or commodification.

3. Time-Decay Mechanism  
Prevents accumulation and maintains circulation of contribution signals.

4. Labor Forecasting & Need Anticipation  
Projects future contribution requirements based on system demand signals.

5. Access Allocation & Redemption  
Transforms contribution signals into access pathways for shared production.

6. Cross-Cooperative & Internodal Reciprocity  
Enables distributed reciprocity across federated cooperative nodes.

7. Fairness, Anti-Coercion & Ethical Safeguards  
Protects against manipulation, coercion, and systemic inequity.

8. Ledger, Transparency & Auditability  
Maintains transparent contribution histories with privacy-respecting auditability.

9. Integration & Coordination  
Coordinates ITC outputs with CDS, OAD, COS, and FRS.

10. Full ITC Orchestration  
Describes system-wide pseudo-code illustrating how modules operate as a unified process.
